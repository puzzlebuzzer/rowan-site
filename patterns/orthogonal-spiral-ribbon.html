Plain language

This design traces a path that turns at right angles while gradually spiraling toward the center. Even though the container is symmetric, the path itself moves asymmetrically through it.

Structural description

The outer form is an eight-pointed star generated by overlapping squares. Inside the container, the ribbon follows a stepwise orthogonal spiral, creating tension between global symmetry and directional transport.

What I was experimenting with

I wanted to explore how a simple path can move through a symmetric structure without following the structure’s own symmetry.

Observations

The ribbon creates a sense of motion inside a rigid container. The contrast between constraint and movement is the point of the piece.
