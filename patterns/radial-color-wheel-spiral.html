Plain language

This design uses wedges arranged in circles that spiral toward the center. The colors follow a repeating rainbow cycle as the spiral progresses inward. Because the spiral cuts across several circular layers, the colors interact in unexpected ways.

Structural description

The pattern consists of concentric circular divisions intersected by a spiral path. The color sequence advances along the spiral rather than around the circles themselves, creating interference between radial and spiral organization.

What I was experimenting with

I wanted to see how a single cyclic sequence behaves when applied along a spiral path instead of around a circle.

Observations

The spiral causes color phases to drift relative to the circular geometry, creating rotating wave-like effects.
